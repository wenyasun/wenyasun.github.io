Bio

Hi! My name is Wenya Sun (孙文雅 in Chinese). I received my master's degree from the Department of Computer Science, Shenzhen University. Currently, I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Computer Science, University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Reynold Cheng and Prof. Wai-ki, Ching.

Research Interests

My research interests are in Data mining, Fintech, and Green Finance. I am deeply passionate about the ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) domain, with a particular focus on ESG investing and sustainability.

Feel free to contact me if you have similar interests.
